The Ping Pong Game

Rules:

Input a number to see all numbers up to that number that are divisible by 3, 5, and 15. They will be replaced by the words ping, pong, and ping-pong respectively.
*Large numbers may take longer to proccess

Please make sure your input had none of the following

  • Contained letters or symbols
  • Contained decimals
  • Contained spaces
  • Contained no input
